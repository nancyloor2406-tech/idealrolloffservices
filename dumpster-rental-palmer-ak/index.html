Palmer, AK Insights: Population,
Zip Codes, Influence, and Service Areas

Palmer (Ahtna: Nił'etse'it'aade or Nuutah; Dena'ina: Denal'i Kena) is a city in and the borough seat of the Matanuska-Susitna Borough, Alaska, United States, located 42 miles (68 km) northeast of Anchorage on the Glenn Highway in the Matanuska Valley. It is the ninth-largest city in Alaska, and forms part of the Anchorage Metropolitan Statistical Area. As of the 2020 census, the population of the city is 5,888, down from 5,937 in 2010.
